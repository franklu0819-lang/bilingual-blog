# Design Specification: Bilingual Technical Blog

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, clean, and developer-focused.
- **Theme**: Support both light and dark modes (system-preferred).
- **Typography**: Sans-serif (Inter) for UI, Serif (Lora) or monospace for content.

## Navigation & UI
- **Navbar**: Sticky navigation with links to Home, Blog, About, and Projects.
- **Language Switcher**: A dropdown or toggle in the Navbar to switch between EN and CN.
- **Blog Feed**: Clean list of post cards with titles, dates, and short descriptions.

## Content Layout
- **Reading Experience**: Wide margins, high contrast, and readable line heights.
- **Code Blocks**: Syntax highlighting (Shiki or Prism).
- **Images**: Responsive, with captions.
